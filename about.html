About Calculator Hub

Calculator Hub is a free, no-signup collection of 14 online calculators covering finance, debt, retirement, health, and everyday math. It was built with one conviction: useful tools shouldn't require an account, shouldn't track you across the web, and should give you an answer in seconds.

What you get

  • Privacy by design. Inputs stay in your browser. The site has no server, so there is nothing to leak.
  • Speed. Static HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Pages load instantly and work offline once visited.
  • Honest recommendations. Affiliate links may appear, but they never change the math. See our Affiliate Disclosure.
  • Currency & theme aware. Money results auto-detect your local currency; switch any time. Dark mode adapts to your system.

How the calculators are built

Each calculator uses standard formulas (the compounding formula, Mifflin-St Jeor for calories, the amortizing payment formula, etc.). Calculations are performed locally in JavaScript; charts are hand-rolled SVG so there are no external chart libraries or weight. We avoid anything that would slow the page down or compromise your inputs.
