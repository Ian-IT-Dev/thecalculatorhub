Sales Tax Calculator

Live — add sales tax to any pre-tax amount, or work out the tax portion of a total.

About sales tax

Sales tax rates vary widely by country, state, county and city. Some places have no sales tax at all. This calculator applies a flat percentage you provide — useful for budgeting a purchase or checking a receipt. For reverse tax (finding the pre-tax amount inside a total), divide the total by (1 + rate/100).
