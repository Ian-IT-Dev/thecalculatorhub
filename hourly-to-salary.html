Hourly to Salary Calculator

Live — convert your hourly wage and weekly hours into weekly, monthly and annual pay.

How annual salary is estimated

This tool multiplies your hourly wage by your weekly hours, then by 52 weeks. It does not account for unpaid time off, overtime multipliers, taxes, or benefits. For a true take-home figure, subtract roughly 20–30% for taxes in many jurisdictions, or use a dedicated tax estimator.
