What's included in a mortgage payment

Your monthly housing payment is more than principal and interest. Lenders often quote PITI: principal, interest, taxes and insurance. This calculator adds HOA dues too, so the headline number reflects what actually leaves your account each month. PMI (private mortgage insurance) is not included — add it to the insurance field if you're putting less than 20% down.

Down payment matters

A larger down payment shrinks your loan, lowers your payment and may eliminate PMI. But don't drain your savings to do it — keep an emergency fund. Compare scenarios with the loan payoff calculator for the bare principal-and-interest math.
