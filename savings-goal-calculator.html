Savings Goal Calculator

Live — work backwards from your target to the monthly amount you need to set aside. Weekly equivalent included for weekly-paid savers.

How to hit a savings goal

A specific dollar goal with a deadline makes saving far more effective than a vague intention. This calculator accounts for both the money you already have and the interest it earns while you save, then solves for the monthly contribution needed to close the gap.

The math

It discounts your goal to today, subtracts your current savings, then amortizes the remaining shortfall across the months you have available. A small expected return meaningfully lowers the required monthly amount over long timelines. Pair this with the compound interest calculator to see how those contributions grow.

Many readers use a high-yield savings account to earn a competitive return while saving toward their goal.
