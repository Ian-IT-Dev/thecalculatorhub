Why use these calculators?

Every calculator here runs entirely in your browser. There is no account to create, no data leaves your device, pages load instantly, and inputs are saved locally so you can come back to them. You can switch currency for money results, toggle dark mode, share a calculation as a link, print results, and export schedules to CSV.

Finance & investing

Compound interest is the most powerful force in personal finance — it's how small, steady contributions turn into meaningful wealth. Our compound interest calculator shows year-by-year growth so you can see the snowball effect. The retirement calculator extends that to a full working career, and the ROI calculator turns any investment outcome into a single comparable number.

Debt & savings

For balances you carry, the loan payoff and mortgage calculators reveal exactly how much interest you pay and how extra payments shorten the term. The credit card payoff calculator shows the true cost of minimum payments. The savings goal calculator works backwards from your target to the monthly amount you need.

Everyday math & health

The tip and percentage tools handle daily math, the BMI and calorie calculators give quick health screening numbers, and the unit converter closes the gap between metric and imperial.
